# HyperAdvancer Fixed-Scope Packages

## Innovation Flash-Diagnosis
**Target:** Executive teams needing rapid outside-in view  
**Duration:** 2-week sprint (remote + on-site)  
**Deliverables:** Organizational readiness scorecard • bottleneck identification map • top-3 quick wins roadmap  

## One-Day Accelerator
**Target:** Corporates wanting hackathon experience without internal capacity  
**Duration:** 6-week preparation + 1 event day  
**Deliverables:** Concept design • speaker curation • facilitator playbook • post-event venturing report  

## Prototype to Pilot
**Target:** Teams with ideas but no Minimal Viable Product  
**Duration:** 3-month agile engagement  
**Deliverables:** Customer discovery research • clickable prototype • lean business case • pilot-site onboarding  

## Innovation Ops Interim Lead
**Target:** Organizations building internal innovation units  
**Duration:** 6-12 months (fractional or full-time)  
**Deliverables:** Roadmap execution • KPI tracking system • coaching to internalize methods  

## Board-Level Advisor
**Target:** Boards seeking foresight and deal flow  
**Duration:** Quarterly retainer  
**Deliverables:** Trend briefings • deal/tech scouting • speaking/PR support  

## Cross-Industry Inspiration Safari
**Target:** Teams stuck in industry silos  
**Duration:** Customized engagement  
**Deliverables:** Curated case studies • partner introductions • "translate to your context" workshop  

## Virtual Hackathon Turbo
**Target:** Global or distributed teams  
**Duration:** 30 days end-to-end  
**Deliverables:** Fuzzy idea to clickable prototype • real user validation • lean business case  

*All packages include knowledge transfer and self-reliance enablement*
